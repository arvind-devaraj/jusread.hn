Older adults know AI is slop. They just like it  (7 pts, 0 comments)
https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48859800

The Hacker News discussion revolves around an article that explores the perception of artificial intelligence (AI) among older adults, particularly in China. The article suggests that while seniors recognize that AI-generated content can be of low quality or "slop," they still find value and enjoyment in using these technologies. 

In the comments, users generally agree that older adults may appreciate the convenience and novelty of AI, despite its shortcomings. Some debate the implications of this acceptance, questioning whether it reflects a lack of critical engagement with technology or simply a different set of priorities among older users. Notable insights include the idea that older adults may prioritize entertainment and ease of use over quality, highlighting a generational divide in how technology is perceived and utilized.
