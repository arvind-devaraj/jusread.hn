I lost my ability to learn anything new because of AI and I need your opinions  (24 pts, 29 comments)
https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47240612

The Hacker News discussion centers around a user's concern that the rise of AI in programming has diminished their ability to learn and appreciate the craftsmanship of coding. They express sadness over the potential loss of understanding fundamental programming concepts, as AI can quickly generate code that feels "good enough," leading to a reliance on these tools rather than a deep engagement with the craft. The user worries that this trend may lead to a decline in code quality and a lack of motivation to learn new languages like Rust.

In the comments, there is a mix of agreement and debate. Some users suggest that the reliance on AI may lead to a loss of critical thinking and problem-solving skills, while others argue that AI can be used as a tool to enhance learning rather than replace it. Notable insights include the idea that understanding concepts remains crucial, even if the syntax or specific language details become less important due to AI assistance. Some commenters advocate for using AI to prototype or assist in coding while still emphasizing the importance of hands-on learning and craftsmanship.

Overall, the discussion reflects a broader concern about the balance between leveraging AI for efficiency and maintaining the integrity and depth of programming skills.
