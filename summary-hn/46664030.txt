Some commenters highlight the importance of distinguishing between human cognition and AI operations, while others debate the definitions and implications of cognition in psychological and neuroscientific contexts. Overall, the discussion underscores a shared concern about the implications of mislabeling AI capabilities, with calls for clearer communication about what AI can and cannot do.
