The Hacker News discussion centers around the launch of Baton, a desktop application designed for developing with AI agents. The creator, who built Baton to streamline the management of multiple AI agents across various IDEs and terminal windows, emphasizes its ability to monitor agent status, switch between workspaces, and quickly spin up new agents in isolated environments.
