Ask HN: Books about Genetic Algorithms  (15 pts, 9 comments)
https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48711408

The Hacker News discussion revolves around a developer seeking recommendations for books on genetic algorithms, expressing a preference for physical copies but also welcoming PDF suggestions. 

In the comments, several users recommend classic texts, with "Genetic Algorithms in Search, Optimization, and Machine Learning" by David E. Goldberg being highlighted as a foundational work, despite its age. Others suggest exploring foundational biology concepts to better understand genetic algorithms and evolutionary algorithms more broadly. There is a distinction made between wanting to learn specifically about genetic algorithms versus evolutionary algorithms in general, with some commenters advocating for a more comprehensive approach. 

Notably, one user expresses interest in the potential of combining evolutionary algorithms with transformer models, indicating a desire for innovative applications in the field. Overall, the discussion emphasizes the importance of foundational knowledge in both genetics and algorithms for a deeper understanding of the subject.
