Show HN: Turn native language audio into flashcards and shadowing practice  (92 pts, 37 comments)
https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48671886

The Hacker News discussion centers around a new language learning tool called LingoChunk, designed to convert native language audio into flashcards and facilitate shadowing practice. The creator, who initially developed the tool for personal use in studying German and Greek, highlights features such as extracting words from audio, generating Anki cards, and providing grammar explanations through AI prompts. The app currently supports 15 input languages and over 30 output languages, with a focus on improving pronunciation for non-native speakers.

In the comments, users express enthusiasm for the tool, sharing their own language learning projects and offering feedback on features like font size adjustments and support for traditional Chinese characters. There is a consensus on the app's potential, with several users praising its user-friendly interface and functionality. Notable insights include discussions about the challenges of language learning resources, the importance of context in vocabulary acquisition, and suggestions for future enhancements, such as automatic pronunciation grading and improved audio generation from text. Overall, the community appreciates the innovative approach to language learning and the creator's willingness to iterate based on user feedback.
