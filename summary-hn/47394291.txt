Tell HN: AI tools are making me lose interest in CS fundamentals  (98 pts, 88 comments)
https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47394291

The Hacker News discussion revolves around a user's concern that the rise of AI coding assistants is diminishing their motivation to study fundamental computer science topics, such as distributed systems and algorithms. The user questions the importance of these fundamentals in an era where AI can quickly generate solutions.

In the comments, there is a consensus that understanding CS fundamentals remains crucial, even with the advent of AI tools. Many commenters argue that knowledge is still power; being able to critically assess AI-generated solutions and understand the underlying concepts is essential for effective use of these tools. Some express concern that relying too heavily on AI could lead to a decline in deep learning and innovation, as users may not develop the necessary skills to evaluate or improve upon AI outputs. Others suggest that AI can actually enhance learning by allowing users to explore concepts more deeply.

Notable insights include the idea that while AI can assist in coding, it cannot replace the need for a solid understanding of the principles behind the code. Commenters emphasize that being able to ask the right questions and understand the AI's limitations is vital for leveraging its capabilities effectively. Overall, the discussion highlights the ongoing relevance of foundational knowledge in computer science, even as AI tools become more prevalent.
