Ask HN: Have you been fired because of AI?  (17 pts, 15 comments)
https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=46867190

The Hacker News discussion titled "Ask HN: Have you been fired because of AI?" seeks personal stories from individuals who have lost their jobs specifically due to the impact of AI, rather than generic corporate restructuring. The original poster is interested in genuine accounts rather than the typical explanations provided in press releases.

In the comments, several users share their experiences and observations regarding job losses linked to AI. Some mention companies halting hiring and pushing employees to adopt AI tools, while others recount being let go due to market conditions influenced by AI. A notable point of debate arises around the distinction between being fired and being made redundant, with some commenters expressing skepticism about companies attributing layoffs to AI when other factors may be at play. Additionally, there are criticisms of companies that claim to have reduced their workforce due to AI while potentially mismanaging their operations.

Overall, the discussion highlights a mix of personal anecdotes and broader reflections on the evolving job landscape as AI continues to reshape industries, with many expressing concern about the implications for job security and the authenticity of corporate narratives surrounding layoffs.
