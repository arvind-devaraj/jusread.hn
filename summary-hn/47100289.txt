Ask HN: What (other) jobs do you think of doing?  (14 pts, 20 comments)
https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47100289

The Hacker News discussion revolves around a user contemplating alternative careers in light of AI's growing impact on various professions, particularly in tech. The original poster expresses a desire for jobs that provide a sense of agency, purpose, and satisfaction, listing options like delivery driver, train driver, electrician, and mechanic.

In the comments, participants share their thoughts on potential career shifts, with many favoring jobs that yield visible results by the end of the day, such as electricians, field technicians, and personal trainers. Some commenters highlight the appeal of trades like HVAC and plumbing for their resistance to automation and decent pay. Others express a longing for more tangible work, with sentiments about the dissatisfaction of purely coding roles and the desire for hands-on, meaningful tasks. Notably, some suggest that jobs involving personal interaction, like teaching or event organizing, will remain valuable despite AI advancements. Overall, the discussion reflects a collective concern about job security in tech and a search for fulfilling alternatives.
