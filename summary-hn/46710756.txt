Show HN: Retain – A unified knowledge base for all your AI coding conversations  (45 pts, 16 comments)
https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=46710756

The Hacker News discussion revolves around a new tool called Retain, developed by Bayram Annakov. Retain is a macOS application designed to serve as a unified knowledge base for users who frequently engage in AI coding conversations with tools like Claude Code, ChatGPT, and Codex. It builds on the earlier CLI tool, claude-reflect, by offering features such as conversation aggregation and instant full-text search, all while keeping user data local in an SQLite database without relying on external servers.

In the comments, users express interest in the tool, with some wishing for cross-platform compatibility, as they are on Windows or Linux. There is a general appreciation for the conversation aggregation and search capabilities, with some users finding it potentially superior to existing tools. However, there is some debate about the "local-first" approach, with questions about its implications and the nature of data handling. Bayram clarifies that the tool does not store user data on external servers, addressing concerns about data privacy.

Overall, the discussion highlights a positive reception for Retain's features, with users eager to try it out, while also raising important questions about data privacy and cross-platform accessibility.
