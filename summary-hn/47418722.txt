The authors suggest that this approach could mimic how organisms adapt to dynamic environments.

The discussion also touches on the limitations of AI's learning capabilities, with some participants noting that real-world consequences of AI failures can be severe, unlike in controlled training environments.

Notable insights include the recognition that while AI can perform tasks traditionally associated with intelligence, its understanding and reasoning capabilities remain limited.
