Show HN: On-device transcriber that's 97% accurate at identifying speakers  (32 pts, 10 comments)
https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48415709

The Hacker News discussion revolves around MimicScribe, a macOS app designed for on-device transcription and speaker identification, boasting 97% accuracy. The creator, Marshall, highlights its features, including real-time meeting talking points, a keyboard- and voice-driven interface, and a focus on privacy through an on-device mode. The app aims to assist users in extracting insights during meetings while maintaining control over their data.

In the comments, users express enthusiasm for the app's capabilities and suggest improvements, such as integrating Ollama for AI functionalities and clarifying system requirements for local performance. A recurring theme is the importance of privacy, with several commenters emphasizing the need for robust privacy measures, especially for a technical audience. Notably, Marshall acknowledges these concerns and is open to exploring additional privacy-focused solutions.

Overall, the discussion reflects a positive reception of MimicScribe, with constructive feedback aimed at enhancing its functionality and addressing privacy issues.
