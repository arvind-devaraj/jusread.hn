AI is making me dumb  (556 pts, 300 comments)
https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48139148

The Hacker News discussion titled "AI is making me dumb" revolves around concerns that reliance on AI tools, particularly in coding and problem-solving, may diminish critical thinking and learning skills. The original post expresses frustration about how some individuals default to AI-generated answers without engaging in their own thought processes.

In the comments, there is a mix of agreement and debate. Some users argue that while AI can enhance productivity, it may lead to a decline in deep understanding and retention of knowledge, especially for junior developers who might not develop the necessary skills if they overly rely on AI. Others counter that AI can be a valuable tool for learning and efficiency, suggesting that it allows for higher-level thinking and abstraction rather than replacing it. Notably, some commenters emphasize the importance of actively engaging with the knowledge generated by AI to retain skills and understanding.

Overall, the discussion highlights a tension between leveraging AI for efficiency and the potential risks of losing foundational skills and critical thinking abilities, particularly among less experienced developers. There is a consensus that while AI can aid in coding and learning, it should not replace the fundamental processes of understanding and problem-solving.
