Launch HN: Agnost AI (YC S26) – Extract user feedback from agent conversations  (23 pts, 8 comments)
https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48908950

The Hacker News discussion centers around Agnost AI, a startup that provides product analytics specifically for chat and voice agents. Founders Shubham and Parth explain that their tool analyzes user conversations to identify behavioral failures, such as users expressing frustration or making repeated requests, which traditional web app metrics do not capture effectively. They emphasize the importance of understanding user intent and frustration to improve AI interactions, offering a demo and detailing their technical approach to clustering conversation data.

In the comments, there is a debate about the value of Agnost AI's service compared to existing tools like Codex, with some users questioning the necessity of paying $499/month for Agnost AI when Codex could potentially perform similar analyses. Others argue that Agnost AI is designed for continuous ingestion of high-volume messages and better integrates user metadata, which Codex does not handle as effectively. Notable insights include discussions on the challenges of identifying user frustration (termed "rageprompting") and the complexities of the UI, with some commenters expressing skepticism about the pricing and usability of the product. Overall, the conversation reflects a mix of curiosity, skepticism, and interest in the niche that Agnost AI aims to fill.
