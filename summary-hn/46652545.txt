OpenAI Introduces Ads to ChatGPT  (18 pts, 5 comments)
https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=46652545

The Hacker News discussion revolves around OpenAI's recent decision to introduce advertisements in ChatGPT, as announced by CEO Sam Altman on Twitter. This move has sparked a variety of reactions among users, reflecting concerns about the implications of ads in a platform that many view as a valuable tool.

In the comments, there is a general sense of discontent and skepticism about the introduction of ads. Users express nostalgia for the days when ads were clearly separated from content, with one commenter suggesting this marks "the beginning of the end" for the platform. The term "enshitification" is used to describe the perceived decline in quality or user experience due to commercialization. Some users also question the rationale behind ads in a non-profit context, indicating a belief that this shift contradicts the original mission of providing accessible AI.

Overall, the discussion highlights a tension between monetization strategies and user expectations, with many commenters fearing that ads could detract from the quality and integrity of the ChatGPT experience.
