Others suggest that the collaboration between tech companies and religious institutions could be seen as a form of "responsibility laundering," where companies seek to improve their image without making substantive changes.

Notable insights include comparisons to other industries that seek ethical guidance while engaging in questionable practices, such as arms dealers consulting religious figures on the morality of their products.
