Ask HN: How have you or your firm made money with LLMs?  (12 pts, 11 comments)
https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=46648752

The Hacker News discussion titled "Ask HN: How have you or your firm made money with LLMs?" invites community members to share specific examples of how they or their companies have successfully monetized large language models (LLMs). The original poster emphasizes the importance of concrete examples that demonstrate market response, rather than vague productivity improvements.

In the comments, several users provide examples of successful LLM applications. One notable case involves a company that improved email engagement through custom messaging generated by LLMs, resulting in significant revenue increases. Others mention using LLMs for automating customer support and content generation, which has led to improved efficiency and allowed teams to focus on strategic tasks. A recurring theme is that LLMs are most effective when integrated into existing workflows rather than marketed as standalone AI solutions.

Notable insights include the idea that LLMs can empower professionals to confidently adjust their pricing structures and enhance their service offerings. Additionally, some commenters express skepticism about the long-term excitement surrounding LLMs, suggesting that the novelty may wear off as users seek more advanced applications beyond current capabilities. Overall, the discussion highlights a growing interest in practical applications of LLMs across various industries.
