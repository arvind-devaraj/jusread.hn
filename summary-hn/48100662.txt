The post discusses the author's experience using AI to create a tool that helps identify what disturbances are waking them up at night. By analyzing noise data, the author discovered that specific sounds, like door slams and motorbikes, were responsible for their sleep disruptions. They also mention improvements in their sleep quality after taking action based on the findings.

In the comments, there is a mix of agreement and skepticism regarding the necessity and complexity of using AI for this purpose. Notably, discussions about CO2 levels in the bedroom and their impact on sleep quality emerge, with some commenters advocating for better ventilation and air quality management.

Overall, the conversation highlights a broader debate about the role of technology in addressing personal health issues, with some advocating for straightforward solutions over high-tech approaches.
