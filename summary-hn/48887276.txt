Show HN: Self-hosted voice AI agent for Asterisk/FreePBX  (25 pts, 11 comments)
https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48887276

The Hacker News discussion revolves around AVA, a self-hosted AI Voice Agent designed for integration with Asterisk/FreePBX, allowing users to maintain control over their voice AI infrastructure. The project, created by the author as a hobby, supports various providers and offers flexibility in building custom pipelines for speech-to-text (STT), text-to-speech (TTS), and large language models (LLMs). It is open-source and aims to provide a user-friendly setup for those looking to avoid vendor lock-in.

In the comments, users inquire about system requirements for running AVA, with the author explaining that a basic setup can handle low call volumes without heavy hardware, while more demanding configurations require GPUs and additional resources. There is also a discussion comparing FreePBX and FusionPBX, with the author clarifying their differences in deployment scenarios. Some users express nostalgia for self-hosted telephony systems, while others highlight the challenges of maintaining such infrastructure in the current landscape dominated by affordable commercial services and spam calls. Overall, the conversation reflects a mix of enthusiasm for the project and skepticism about the viability of self-hosted solutions in today's market.
