Show HN: Nyx – multi-turn, adaptive, offensive testing harness for AI agents  (20 pts, 8 comments)
https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47827802

The Hacker News discussion revolves around Nyx, an autonomous testing harness designed to identify failure modes in AI agents, which often break in ways that traditional software does not. The tool aims to uncover logic bugs, instruction-following failures, and edge cases through multi-turn adaptive conversations and multi-modal testing, all while operating in a blackbox manner. Nyx promises to find issues in under 10 minutes, significantly faster than manual audits.

In the comments, users express a mix of enthusiasm and skepticism. Some appreciate the innovation and acknowledge the challenges of evaluating AI behavior, while others question whether Nyx adds unnecessary complexity. A notable point of discussion is the comparison between Nyx and traditional fuzzing techniques, with one commenter suggesting that insights from fuzzing literature could enhance Nyx's approach. Additionally, users inquire about integrating Nyx into CI/CD pipelines, indicating interest in practical applications of the tool. Overall, the conversation highlights both the potential benefits and challenges of testing AI agents effectively.
