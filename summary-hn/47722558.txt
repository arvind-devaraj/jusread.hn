Why Isn't Everything Different Yet? (AI, where are you?)  (15 pts, 8 comments)
https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47722558

The Hacker News discussion revolves around an article questioning why the transformative effects of AI are not yet evident in various sectors. The author explores the potential of AI and compares its current state to the early days of electrical motors, suggesting that while AI may not seem revolutionary now, its gradual integration could lead to significant changes over time.

In the comments, users debate the tangible benefits AI has brought so far, with some expressing skepticism about its current impact and others drawing parallels to historical technological advancements. A key point of contention is whether AI is merely accelerating startup creation or if it holds the potential for more substantial innovations. Some commenters highlight that many processes have already been automated, suggesting that future AI advancements may yield incremental improvements rather than groundbreaking changes.

Notable insights include the idea that the legal and infrastructural frameworks surrounding technology can hinder rapid adoption, as seen in the early days of internet commerce. Additionally, there is a recognition that while AI may not yet be delivering dramatic results, its long-term potential could lead to significant enhancements across various industries.
