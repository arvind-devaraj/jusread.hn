Ask HN: ADHD – How do you manage the constant stream of thoughts and ideas?  (124 pts, 141 comments)
https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=46610156

The Hacker News discussion revolves around a user seeking advice on managing ADHD, particularly the overwhelming influx of thoughts and ideas that disrupt focus and productivity. The user shares their struggles with maintaining concentration while running a business and balancing personal life, and they inquire about others' experiences and coping strategies.

In the comments, several users resonate with the original poster's experiences, noting that many founders and creatives exhibit ADHD-like traits, such as intense curiosity and difficulty with follow-through. Common strategies discussed include using organizational tools, time-boxing tasks, and minimizing distractions in one's environment. Some commenters emphasize the importance of sleep, exercise, and medication, while others highlight the need for externalizing thoughts through note-taking.

Notable insights include the idea that ADHD brains may be more adept at meditation due to their habitual need for refocusing, and the acknowledgment that while tools can help manage symptoms, they are often compensatory rather than addressing the underlying issues. The conversation also touches on the complexities of medication, with users sharing personal experiences and preferences regarding various ADHD treatments.
