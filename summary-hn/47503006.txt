So where are all the AI apps?  (452 pts, 399 comments)
https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47503006

The Hacker News discussion titled "So where are all the AI apps?" explores the current landscape of applications created using AI technologies. The original post raises questions about the visibility and prevalence of AI-driven applications, prompting users to share their experiences and creations.

In the comments, there is a mix of perspectives. Some users, like superkuh, highlight the personal empowerment AI tools provide, enabling individuals to create numerous applications for their own needs without corporate involvement. Others, such as ramesh31, argue that games could be a significant area for AI applications, although there is skepticism about the quality of AI-generated games. Many commenters agree that while AI can facilitate app development, the quality and enjoyment of the resulting products vary widely, with a consensus that most AI-generated content tends to be subpar.

Notable insights include the idea that AI tools have lowered the barrier for creating personalized applications, allowing users to build solutions for specific tasks quickly. This shift is seen as a new frontier in app development, where even simple, one-off applications can be created in a fraction of the time it would traditionally take. Overall, the discussion reflects both excitement and caution regarding the potential of AI in application development.
