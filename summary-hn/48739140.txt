The blog post "Local Reasoning for Global Properties" discusses the importance of local reasoning in software development, emphasizing how local proofs and checks can help ensure global properties of a program. The author argues that by focusing on smaller, localized contexts, developers can create clearer and more maintainable code, which can be verified more easily.
