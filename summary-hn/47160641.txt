Ask HN: What's it like working in big tech recently with all the AI tools?  (28 pts, 15 comments)
https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47160641

The Hacker News discussion revolves around the impact of AI coding tools on day-to-day work in big tech companies. The original poster seeks insights on whether these tools have accelerated development, affected code quality, and influenced team productivity.

Many commenters agree that the use of AI tools has led to a faster pace of development, with some reporting significant increases in pull requests (PRs). However, there is a consensus that this speed comes at the cost of code quality, with an uptick in bugs and a tendency for code reviews to become more superficial. Some users express concern about the shift in job expectations, where developers are now seen more as editors of AI-generated code rather than creators, leading to increased mental fatigue and decision-making challenges.

Notable insights include the idea that while AI tools can enhance productivity, they also introduce complexities in code integration and architectural decisions. Some commenters highlight a growing dependency on AI, which may hinder the development of a deep understanding of codebases. Overall, the discussion reflects a mix of enthusiasm for the potential of AI tools and caution regarding their implications for software development practices.
