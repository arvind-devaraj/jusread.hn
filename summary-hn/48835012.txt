Show HN: Requirements Engineering with Formal Verification  (27 pts, 4 comments)
https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48835012

The Hacker News discussion centers around the launch of an open-source formal methods system called Fizzbee, which focuses on requirements engineering through formal verification. The application aims to enhance the software development process by asking insightful follow-up questions, converting prompts into formal specifications, identifying complex requirements gaps, and generating validation scenarios. Ultimately, it produces a shareable specification document that can lead to more efficient coding iterations.

In the comments, users express positive feedback about the tool's utility, particularly highlighting its effectiveness in identifying edge cases during the requirements elicitation phase. One commenter suggests adding the ability to export finalized specifications to standard formats like Markdown or Jira tickets, indicating a desire for improved integration with existing workflows. Overall, the discussion reflects enthusiasm for the tool's potential and a few constructive suggestions for enhancements.
