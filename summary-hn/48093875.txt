Using AI for just 10 minutes might make you lazy and dumb  (29 pts, 46 comments)
https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48093875

The Hacker News discussion revolves around a Wired article that suggests using AI, even for just 10 minutes, could lead to laziness and diminished cognitive abilities, particularly among younger individuals still developing foundational skills. The conversation highlights concerns about the implications of AI in educational settings and the need for societal discussions on its regulation.

Commenters generally agree that while established professionals may not feel the effects of AI as strongly, younger users could be at risk of becoming overly reliant on it, potentially stunting their critical thinking and problem-solving skills. There is a debate about whether using AI equates to "outsourcing" one's thinking or if it can be a tool for enhancing understanding and creativity. Some argue that the issue lies in how individuals engage with AI, emphasizing the importance of maintaining critical thinking skills. Notably, several commenters express a sense of urgency for educational institutions to adapt and address these challenges, while others reflect on their personal experiences with AI, illustrating both its benefits and drawbacks.
