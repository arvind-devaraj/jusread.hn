Instead, it focuses on constraint satisfaction over conserved quantities to produce behaviors akin to those found in biological neural circuits, such as hysteresis and bistable memory.

Recommendations for further reading on neuromorphic computing are shared, highlighting foundational texts and current advancements in the field.
