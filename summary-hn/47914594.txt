Ask HN: Anyone want to collaborate on a local-first AI-based research assistant  (24 pts, 9 comments)
https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47914594

In a recent Hacker News discussion, a sophomore named Venkatram seeks collaborators for his project aimed at creating a local-first AI-based research assistant. His goal is to develop a tool that transforms documents into easily reusable research assets, contrasting with proprietary solutions. He emphasizes the need for optimization, particularly for users with lower-end computers, and shares a GitHub link to his project, Gigabook-LM.

The comments reveal interest from several users, including one who is currently a software development engineer and expresses a desire to collaborate. Another commenter compares Venkatram's project to "AnythingLLM," noting that while both aim to optimize AI usage, Venkatram's approach prioritizes logic over user interface, which he believes makes it more accessible. Additionally, another user mentions their own project related to building a local knowledge base, suggesting potential synergies. Overall, the discussion highlights a shared enthusiasm for developing more efficient and accessible AI tools.
