OpenAI will start testing ads in ChatGPT free and Go tiers  (21 pts, 13 comments)
https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=46659990

The Hacker News discussion centers around OpenAI's announcement that it will begin testing advertisements in the free and Go tiers of ChatGPT. This move is seen as a way for OpenAI to recover financial losses, as several commenters speculate about the company's financial struggles and the implications of introducing ads.

The main points of debate include concerns about "enshittification," a term used to describe the degradation of user experience due to intrusive ads. Some commenters argue that adding ads to a previously ad-free service is a clear example of this phenomenon, while others believe that ads are a reasonable trade-off for free services. There is also discussion about the potential influence of user queries on the types of ads displayed, raising questions about privacy and data usage.

Notable opinions include skepticism about the future of free AI services, with some users expressing a desire to delete their accounts in protest. Others highlight the competitive landscape, noting that OpenAI is under pressure from both free alternatives and well-funded competitors. Overall, the conversation reflects a mix of frustration, nostalgia for ad-free experiences, and a recognition of the financial realities facing tech companies.
