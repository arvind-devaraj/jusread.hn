The Hacker News post introduces "Yochai," an AI-powered Socratic learning guide designed to facilitate the study of Jewish texts. It utilizes a comprehensive library of over 1,000 primary sources from the Jewish canon, along with a vast knowledge graph containing millions of entities and relationships. Yochai encourages users to engage deeply with the texts, fostering personal insights and understanding. The creators have also open-sourced their knowledge graph to allow others to build upon their work.
