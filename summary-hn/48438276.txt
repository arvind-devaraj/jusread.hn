The Hacker News discussion revolves around an article that explains the concept of tokens in large language models (LLMs). The author emphasizes that tokens are not simply characters or words but rather unique units specific to each model, which the model perceives as integer indices in its own vocabulary. This distinction is crucial for understanding how LLMs process language.
