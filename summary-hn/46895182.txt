Ask HN: Does anyone have interests in anything besides AI?  (11 pts, 7 comments)
https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=46895182

The Hacker News discussion titled "Ask HN: Does anyone have interests in anything besides AI?" explores whether the tech community's focus on artificial intelligence overshadows other interests and fields. The original post prompts users to share their passions outside of AI.

In the comments, participants express a variety of interests, including astronomy, cryptocurrency, climate change, behavioral health, and even hands-on activities like woodworking and metalworking. A common sentiment is that while AI is currently a hot topic, many longstanding problems in other fields remain unresolved or inadequately addressed. Some commenters emphasize a preference for traditional problem-solving methods over reliance on AI tools, highlighting a desire for deeper engagement with complex issues rather than simply chasing the latest trends.

Notably, one user mentions their commitment to the Linux from Scratch project, indicating a dedication to foundational computing skills. Overall, the discussion reflects a broader perspective on technology and innovation, suggesting that there is still significant interest in diverse areas beyond AI.
