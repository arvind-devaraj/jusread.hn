Meta creating AI version of Mark Zuckerberg so staff can talk to the boss  (11 pts, 6 comments)
https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47755334

The Hacker News discussion revolves around Meta's initiative to create an AI version of CEO Mark Zuckerberg, allowing employees to interact with a digital representation of him. This move has sparked a variety of reactions among commenters.

Many users express skepticism and humor regarding the idea, with some suggesting that the AI could serve as a platform for employees to vent frustrations about management decisions. Others question the necessity of keeping the real Zuckerberg around if an AI version could handle interactions, hinting at potential cost-saving measures for shareholders. There’s also a playful commentary on Zuckerberg's evolution from being perceived as an "alien" to a "robot" and now an "AI," with one user sarcastically suggesting that the next step could be a ghost.

Overall, the discussion highlights a mix of skepticism, humor, and critique regarding the implications of replacing human interaction with AI in corporate settings.
