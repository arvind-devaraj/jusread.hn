Show HN: LemonSlice – Upgrade your voice agents to real-time video  (133 pts, 133 comments)
https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=46783600

The Hacker News discussion centers around LemonSlice, a platform that enables users to create interactive video avatars for real-time conversations. The co-founders introduced their new model, Lemon Slice 2, which can generate video at 20 frames per second on a single GPU. They believe that video avatars will become the predominant form of conversational AI, as people generally prefer visual content over text. The discussion includes a demo and a playground for users to experiment with the technology.

In the comments, users expressed excitement about the potential applications of LemonSlice, particularly in creating more engaging AI assistants and training scenarios. There was a consensus on the impressive technological advancements since the previous version, with many praising the engineering behind the real-time capabilities. However, some commenters raised concerns about the ethical implications and potential misuse of such technology, emphasizing the need for responsible use. Notable insights included discussions about integrating LemonSlice with other AI tools and the desire for more control over avatar expressions and movements. Overall, the community showed enthusiasm for the innovation while also contemplating its broader impact.
