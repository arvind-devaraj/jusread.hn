Stop prompting. Let the AI interview you to build specs  (18 pts, 15 comments)
https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=47059652

The Hacker News discussion revolves around a tool called IdeaForge, which aims to help non-technical users effectively communicate their project specifications to AI by conducting an interview-style process. The creator, enha, shares their experience of struggling to articulate project requirements and highlights how IdeaForge automates the questioning process to reduce cognitive load for users.

In the comments, there is a consensus on the importance of clear communication with AI, with several users agreeing that prompting the AI to ask questions can lead to better outcomes. Some commenters express concerns about privacy and the need for a formal privacy policy, which enha promptly addresses. Others compare IdeaForge to existing tools, noting its focus on the pre-development phase and its potential benefits for non-developers. However, some experienced developers feel that the tool may not fully meet their needs, indicating a divide in its target audience.

Notable insights include the recognition that many users struggle with defining their project requirements, and the suggestion that a more streamlined user experience could enhance IdeaForge's effectiveness. Overall, the discussion highlights both the potential and limitations of using AI for project specification, particularly for those without a technical background.
