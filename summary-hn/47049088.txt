In the comments, there is a consensus that AI writing tends to produce generic content, with users expressing frustration over the loss of "pointiness" and individuality in prose. Some commenters refer to this phenomenon as a "race to the middle" or "the great blur," highlighting a perceived decline in quality and creativity.
