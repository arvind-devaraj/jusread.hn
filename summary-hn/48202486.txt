Alternatives to HN for "tech outside of AI" discussion?  (61 pts, 38 comments)
https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48202486

The Hacker News discussion revolves around the perception that AI-related topics have overwhelmingly dominated the platform, overshadowing other tech discussions. The original poster expresses a desire for a return to a more diverse range of topics, particularly those showcasing innovative projects, and seeks alternatives to HN or ways to filter out AI content.

In the comments, many users agree that HN has become overly focused on AI, with some suggesting that it has turned into a marketing outlet for AI products. Others acknowledge that while AI discussions have always existed, the current saturation is unprecedented. Several users recommend alternatives like Lobste.rs, which they feel maintains a higher quality of discussion and allows for better content filtering. Some also propose tools and extensions to help filter out AI-related posts on HN.

Notable insights include the acknowledgment that the tech landscape is shifting towards AI, leading to a perception that non-AI topics are becoming less relevant. Additionally, some commenters reflect on their experiences with HN, noting a decline in the community's support for indie projects and a preference for mainstream content. Overall, the discussion highlights a tension between the evolving focus of tech discussions and the desire for a more varied and engaging discourse.
