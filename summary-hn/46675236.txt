Show HN: Intent Layer: A context engineering skill for AI agents  (29 pts, 5 comments)
https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=46675236

The Hacker News discussion revolves around a blog post titled "Intent Layer: A context engineering skill for AI agents," which introduces a framework for enhancing AI agents' understanding of context and intent. The author, Hunter17, explains how this framework allows for better management of AI interactions by precomputing constraints and maintaining clarity on the agent's scope and authority.

In the comments, there is a notable emphasis on crediting the original source of the idea, with user moonshotideas praising the clarity of the post while urging acknowledgment of the foundational work from Intent Systems. Hunter17 responds positively, confirming that a credits section has been added to the blog and will also be included in the GitHub repository. Another commenter, cadamsdotcom, questions how the intent layer differs from simply asking the agent to document its processes. Hunter17 clarifies that the intent layer employs principles of progressive disclosure and aims to prevent "intent drift" by regularly updating the agent's understanding of its goals.

Overall, the discussion highlights the importance of proper attribution in the tech community while exploring innovative approaches to improving AI agent functionality.
