Show HN: NanoEuler – GPT-2 scale model in pure C/CUDA from scratch  (55 pts, 26 comments)
https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48710778

The Hacker News discussion revolves around a project called NanoEuler, which is a text generation model built from scratch using pure C/CUDA. The creator, motivated by a desire to understand the inner workings of large language models (LLMs), aims to explore the relationship between model parameters, data, and GPU optimization. They started with a small model trained on Shakespeare's texts and are seeking feedback and suggestions from the community.

In the comments, there are mixed reactions regarding the coding style and the clarity of the code, with some users questioning whether parts of it were generated by an LLM. Others express interest in the project's potential and the learning experience it offers. A notable point of debate is the performance of NanoEuler compared to established frameworks like PyTorch, with the creator acknowledging that while their implementation is slower, it provides valuable insights into LLM development.

Overall, the discussion highlights the challenges and learning opportunities in building LLMs from the ground up, as well as the community's curiosity about the project's technical aspects and the creator's approach to optimization.
