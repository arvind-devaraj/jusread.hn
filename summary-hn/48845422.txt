Several commenters suggest that a demo video would enhance understanding of the tool's capabilities, which Ronak acknowledges and promises to work on.

Notable insights include the recognition of the tool's potential for simplifying video editing workflows and the excitement around the live editing capabilities where both AI and humans can collaborate.
