Tell HN: I'm tired of AI-generated answers  (120 pts, 51 comments)
https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48230104

The Hacker News discussion centers around a user's frustration with the prevalence of AI-generated responses in online interactions. The user shares experiences where they received unhelpful AI answers to serious questions, only to find that others were also forwarding AI-generated content without engaging meaningfully. This led to a broader sentiment of wanting genuine human communication over AI-generated responses.

In the comments, many users express agreement with the original poster's concerns, lamenting the dilution of quality in online discourse due to AI. Some suggest that reliance on AI responses can lead to a lack of critical thinking and genuine engagement, while others propose solutions like forming small, trusted communities to foster real interactions. Notable insights include the idea that the rise of AI-generated content may force people to seek more face-to-face communication, as online interactions become less trustworthy. There is also a call for accountability and transparency regarding AI-generated content, with suggestions for reputation systems to distinguish between human and AI contributions.
