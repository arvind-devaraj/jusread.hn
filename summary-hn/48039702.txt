Time to add option in Hacker News "AI excluded Show HN"  (19 pts, 6 comments)
https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=48039702

The Hacker News discussion revolves around a user's suggestion to add an option to exclude AI-related content from the site, expressing frustration with the prevalence of AI projects and updates. The user feels that the focus on AI is diminishing their interest in Hacker News.

In the comments, there is a mix of agreement and debate. Some users acknowledge that the quality of submissions has improved due to stricter rules around AI-related content, while others argue that the essence of Hacker News—intellectual curiosity and meaningful discussions—may be compromised when projects heavily rely on AI. A few commenters share their experiences, noting that non-AI projects struggle to gain traction in the current landscape dominated by AI content.

Notably, some users suggest existing tools and methods to filter out AI-related posts, indicating that while the desire for an official exclusion option exists, there are already workarounds available for those seeking to avoid AI discussions.
